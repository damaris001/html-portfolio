My Best K-Drama Movies 2023 According to Damaris

My Top 3 Movies

My Liberation Notes

This movie made me cry and brought me back to reality, because the truth is life is complex and tough but the end may or may not be fine. Just be ready to accept it.

Our Beloved Summer

This movie showed me that love can stand the test of time even when things are tiring and crazy. It may take time but it will find its way to you.

Uncontrollably Fond

This one made me cry too. Knowing that life is unpredictable, I want to love and loved to the fullest.
